Indomitable Opulance

Sandton City / AirBnB
Project 01

The design concept for this particular interior space derives from one of Pebetse’s (the client) hobbies which would be skydiving. I looked up on what mental/psychological benefits come from skydiving and I was quite intrigued, it enhances one’s mental clarity, it improves one’s mood, and it also improves sleep, etc. I want the space/interior of this apartment to stimulate those feelings, through the colour palette that is chosen, through adding naturals elements such as timber (wood) and indoor plants; and adding round shapes which can help make one happier.
